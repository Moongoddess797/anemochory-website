About Rachel Schmalzried

Rachel Schmalzried is a strategist and program leader with over 15 years of experience guiding ideas from inception to impact. She has built her career at the intersection of innovation, technology, and organizational growth—helping teams secure funding, shape business plans, execute R&D programs, and manage the operational demands of new products.

Her work spans startups, nonprofits, and Fortune-scale organizations across aerospace and defense, healthcare/SaaS, and social impact. Whether supporting early-stage founders, managing complex IP portfolios, or leading product roadmaps, Rachel blends strategic vision with hands-on execution.

She has helped founders identify and secure funding that unlocked additional backing and positioned companies for acquisition. Through her leadership in proposal development, she has generated multimillions in funding, scaled small teams into high-performing organizations (and successfully ramped them down post-mission), supported both emerging and large-scale operations, and guided the launch of products ranging from hardware APIs to SaaS platforms. Along the way, Rachel has earned a reputation for diligence, perseverance, and driving results from complexity.

Rachel also brings creativity and community to everything she does—applying her detail-driven approach in unexpected places, including the world of weddings.
