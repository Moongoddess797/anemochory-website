About Rachel Schmalzried

Rachel Schmalzried is the founder and CEO of Anemochory, LLC, a Bay Area–based consulting and innovation studio founded in 2014. Based in San Jose, she leads the company’s strategic direction and operations.

She most recently also acted as the Director of Intellectual Property & Senior Technical Product Manager at Satelles in Palo Alto prior to their acquisition by Iridium Satellite Communications, where she led technical product development for their R&D team and intellectual property strategy for the company.

Rachel earned advanced degrees from the University of Southern California—including an MBA in Entrepreneurship and an M.S. in Systems Engineering with an Aerospace focus—and holds a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering from Pennsylvania State University.

Outside of work, Rachel has also taken on the role of Chief Memory Maker & Master Planner — building big ideas and toddler adventures, one schedule at a time.
