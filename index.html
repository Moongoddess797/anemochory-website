About Anemochory

Previously focused on consulting work only, we are currently developing a new product in stealth mode.

What is Anemochory? We are often given perplexed looks when introducing the name. We don't take offense. You may not know us, but we know our jobs—and to us, that’s more important. By definition, Anemochory is seed dispersal by the wind. One well-known example is when a child blows dandelion seeds, watching their wishes scatter in the turbulent flow of their breath and the wind.

We act as the breeze to help propel your company where it wants to go. In a way, we make wishes come true. How many consulting firms can say that?
